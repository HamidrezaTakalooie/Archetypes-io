Archetypes

Archetype meaning in dictionary is "a very typical example of a certain person or thing" but in terms of psycology we consider archetypes as the term which means "The original pattern in ancient Greek".

Jung used the concept of archetype in his theory of the human psyche. He identified 15 universal, mythic characters archetypes,8 for men and 7 for women. reside within our collective unconscious.
